ingredients

Serving: 1

  • 1/2 long-grain rice of jasmine rice
  • peanut or vegetable oil, divided
  • 3 large eggs, beated to blend, 5 cloves garlic, grated of finely chopped
  • (1") piece ginger, peeled, grated or finely chopped
  • 4 scallions, white, pale green, and dark green parts separated, thiny sliced 2 tbsp. reduced Sodiumsoy sauce
  • 1 tsp. granylated sugar
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp. white pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. MSG
  • 1/3 c. unsalted butter, cut into pieces

Direction

  1. Step 1 Fill a large pot about halfway with water 3 minutes, then drain with water and bring to a boil. meanwhile, throughtoutly rinse the rice in a strainer until water runs almost clear.
  2. Step 2 cook rice in boiling water 3 minutes, then drain with a fine-mesh strainer that fins in pot. wipe out pot, pour in about 2 water, and bring to a simmer. With rice in strainer, make a few divots in rice through to bottom of strainer with a chopstick or an butter knife.
  3. Step 3 Cover strainer with foil, then place in pot over simmering water. place pot lid over strainer, if there's a lot of stream eascaping. wrap foil or a damp tea towel around rim of the potand strainer.
  4. Step 4 The steam until rice is cooked through but still slightly al dente, about 15 minutes, it should have a drier feel than normal steamed rice. spread onto a baking sheet and let cool to room temperture. refrigerate until ready to use.
  5. Step 5 Heat a large wok or well-seasonal cast-iron skillet over high heat. Pour in 1 tablespoon oil and swirl wok to coat. Once oil is shimmering, add eggs and cook, breaking up into small pieces, until cooked through but not browning, about 1 minute. Transfer to a plate. wipe out wok.
  6. Step 6 In same wok over high heat, swirl remaining 2 tablespoons oil. cook garlic and ginlic begins to brown about 30 seconds. Add rice and immediately ross to combine. Fry rice, stirring contantly, until no clumps remain and you begin to hear some light popping sounds from rice toasting, 2 to 3 minutes.
  7. Step 7 Add soy sauce, granulated sugar, salt, pepper, and MSG and cook, tossing contanly, until soy sauce is absorbed, about 1 minuteadd eggs, peas, and dark green scallion parts and cook, tossing contantly, until warmed through and incorporated, about 2 minutes more. Add butter and coo, tossing, until melted, about 30 seconds more.
